About Us

History and tradition, Practice-oriented educational institution leading IT education
Company introduction and purpose of establishment
This educational institution was founded in 1983 as the first venture company, leading the digital era.
Established by Bit Computer Co. , Ltd. for the purpose of [the more technology is shared, the larger] [to cultivate excellent human resources and give back to society] .
Therefore, the head of the agency understands and empathizes with the direction of government policy as the best IT expert in Korea.
We operate high-quality training courses to cultivate human resources that industries need.
In addition, for the purpose of sound development and promotion of the domestic software industry, such as the activities of the chairman of the Korea Software Industry Association.
We are engaged in various activities to raise awareness of the software industry and to improve the software industry policy and system.
Main business and performance
